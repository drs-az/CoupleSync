How It Works

Getting Started

Step 1: Generate Individual Profiles
Copy the Individual Prompt into your preferred LLM—choose one of: ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Meta AI, or Grok. Then, each partner should generate their own psychological profile.

Step 2: Create Your Relationship Profile
Working together, copy the Couple Prompt into your LLM. Keep both individual profiles handy—you’ll be asked to paste them. The LLM will output your CoupleSync relationship profile.

Step 3: Start Coaching
Copy the Coaching Prompt into your LLM on your device. Paste your individual profiles and your relationship profile into the chat. You now have a fully informed, 24/7 AI relationship coach ready with actionable advice.
